## Summary
***
> [!quote] Summary in a few sentences
> > This document outlines the product requirements for an AI-powered Personal Knowledge Management (PKM) agent that leverages a knowledge graph and Model Context Protocol (MCP) to help startup founders, PMs, and solo founders organize, and utilize their knowledge base to achieve their business goals.

## Content
***
### 1. Introduction
#### 1.1. Purpose
The purpose of this product is to provide a "second brain" for startup founders, PMs, and solo founders, helping them to not only manage their personal and professional knowledge but also to leverage it to make better decisions and accelerate their progress towards their goals.
#### 1.2. Background & Rationale
Today's knowledge workers are overwhelmed with information. They use a variety of tools to manage their notes, bookmarks, and documents, but these tools are often disconnected, making it difficult to find and synthesize information. The proposed PKM agent will solve this problem by providing 'a unified interface to a user's personal knowledge graph, powered by AI and MCP.
#### 1.3. Goals & Objectives
*   **Primary Goal:** To create a PKM agent that helps users achieve their goals by providing them with the right information at the right time.
*   **Business Goal:** To generate $20,000 in revenue within the first 1-2 months of launch.
*   **User-centric Goal:** To reduce the time users spend searching for information by 50%.

### 2. Target Audience & User Personas
*   **Target Audience:** Startup founders, Product Managers (PMs), and solo founders.
*   **User Persona: "Alex, the Solo Founder"**
    *   **Needs:** To quickly validate ideas, create a go-to-market strategy, and manage all aspects of the business.
    *   **Pain Points:** Feeling overwhelmed with information, difficulty prioritizing tasks, and a lack of a unified view of their business.

### 3. Features & Functionality
#### 3.1. High-Level Features
*   **Knowledge Graph Building & GraphRAG**: Automatically build a knowledge graph from Obsidian/Notion and perform structured reasoning over connected concepts.
*   **n8n Automation**: Capability to generate, configure, and launch automated workflows using n8n.
*   **Deep Research & Lead Gen**: AI-driven deep research (via Perplexity/Search) and automated lead generation for business growth.
*   **Action-Oriented Agents**:
    *   **Email & Scheduling**: Automated handling of communications and calendar management.
    *   **Note-Taking & Synthesis**: seamless integration with Obsidian and Notion.
    *   **Visual Output**: Generation of infographics and Obsidian `.canvas` files for spatial mapping.
*   **Structured Reasoning**: Integrated multi-step thinking models for complex problem-solving.
*   **Vibe-Coding Interface**: A high-velocity, natural language-first development and execution style.

#### 3.2. Detailed Requirements (User Stories)
*   **As a user, I want to be able to connect my Obsidian vault to the PKM agent so that I can build a knowledge graph from my notes.**
*   **As a user, I want to be able to ask the PKM agent questions in natural language so that I can quickly find the information I need.**
*   **As a user, I want to be able to use an "n8n Agent" to generate a workflow JSON so that I can automate repetitive business tasks.**
*   **As a user, I want the agent to use GraphRAG to connect insights from my meeting notes and market research clippings.**

### 4. Go-to-Market Strategy
#### 4.1. Marketing Channels
*   **Content Marketing:** Create high-quality blog posts, tutorials, and videos that demonstrate the value of the PKM agent.
*   **Social Media Marketing:** Engage with the target audience on Twitter and other social media platforms.
*   **Community Engagement:** Participate in relevant online communities and forums.
#### 4.2. Launch Plan
*   **Phase 1: Pre-launch (Month 1)**
    *   Create a landing page to capture email addresses.
    *   Start building a community on Twitter.
*   **Phase 2: Launch (Month 2)**
    *   Launch the product to the email list and social media followers.
    *   Offer a limited-time discount to early adopters.

### 5. Monetization & Packaging
#### 5.1. Pricing Model
*   **One time purchase on Gumroad:** paid package for one time purchase.
*   **Pro Tier ($20):**
    *   Knowledge graph creation for multiple Obsidian and Notion workspaces.
    *   Advanced AI-powered search.
    *   Access to all agents.

### 6. Success Metrics (KPIs)
*   **Revenue:** $20,000 in the first 2 months.
*   **Number of Users:** 1,000 paid users in the first 2 months.
*   **User Engagement:** 50% of users are active on a weekly basis.
